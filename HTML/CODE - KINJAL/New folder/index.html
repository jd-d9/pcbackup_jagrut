Watts Health Center

Watts Health Center is headquarters for medical and administrative services offered by the Watts Healthcare Corporation. The center, one of the first community health centers in the United States, is a 63,000 square-foot facility that houses a staff of 300 and offers a full range of services including clinical, preventive, specialty, and ancillary services.

Same Day services include adult medicine, obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics and urgent care.

Ancillary and Support services include Radiology (X-Ray and Ultrasound), Laboratory services, referrals, and substance abuse treatment support..
